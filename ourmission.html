About Us

We aim to please God by creating an eco-friendly environment. We were given only one earth, and it seems that our ecosystem is suffering now more than ever with burning of fossile fuels. This is why our facilities are completely powered by human waste. Waste is a renewable source that nobody has thought to use for power because of its name, "waste."
