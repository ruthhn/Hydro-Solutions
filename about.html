What We Do

We here at Hydro Solutions work to find solutions to environmental problems while also providing the world with energy using our patented machine, known as CANA, we are able to turn sewage into clean water, and turn the waste into energy. This means that we can power cities using what we already have.


What is the CANA?

CANA was the where the wedding that Jesus went to was held. If you are unfamiliar with the story, Jesus turned water into wine. In a way, we are "turning water into wine," but instead of water and wine, we are turning sewage back into water.
